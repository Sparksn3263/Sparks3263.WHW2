Overview

"The Matrix" is a groundbreaking 1999 science fiction film that explores themes
of reality, control, and human freedom, featuring a dystopian world where humanity
is unknowingly trapped in a simulated reality created by machines.

Written and Directed by: The Wachowskis

Cast

  1. Keanu Reeves as Neo
  2. Laurence Fishburne as Morpheus
  3. Carrie-Anne Moss as Trinity
  4. Hugo Weaving as Agent Smith
  5. Gloria Foster as The Oracle
  6. Joe Pantoliano as Cypher
